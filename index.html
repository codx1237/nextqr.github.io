Frequently Asked Questions

Is NextQR free to use?

Yes, NextQR is completely free to download and use. There are no hidden fees or in-app purchases required to access all features.

Does NextQR work offline?

Absolutely! NextQR generates QR codes directly on your device, so you don't need an internet connection to create them.

Can I customize the appearance of my QR codes?

Yes, NextQR offers various customization options including color selection, size adjustment, and the ability to add logos or images to your QR codes.

What types of content can I encode?

NextQR supports multiple content types including URLs, plain text, contact information (vCard), Wi-Fi network details, email addresses, phone numbers, and SMS messages.

Is my data secure with NextQR?

Yes, privacy is our top priority. All QR code generation happens locally on your device, and we don't collect or store any of your personal information.
